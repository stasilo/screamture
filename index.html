SCREAMture!

This is the Screamture

A 3d-printed sculpture generated using sound recorded through your microphone.

Start a recording and speak, scream or make other noises, then export your newly generated sculpture as an STL-model ready for 3d-printing in software lika Cura. You can move the model around and zoom in and out after recording by using your mouse.

Note
This only works in browsers with WebRTC (Chrome, Edge and Firefox) and WebGL support. You'll also need to give permissions for micrphone access - look for a popup near the address bar.
